Pasta
Ingredients: Pasta, Tomato Sauce, Cheese
Instructions: Boil the pasta, add tomato sauce, mix well, and top with cheese.

Grilled Sandwich
Ingredients: Bread, Butter, Cheese, Tomato
Instructions: Butter the bread, add cheese and tomato, then grill until golden brown.

Fried Rice
Ingredients: Rice, Vegetables, Soy Sauce, Oil
Instructions: Stir-fry vegetables, add cooked rice, pour soy sauce, and cook for 5 minutes.

Omelette
Ingredients: Eggs, Onion, Salt, Pepper
Instructions: Beat eggs, mix with chopped onion, season, and cook in a pan.

Fruit Salad
Ingredients: Apple, Banana, Orange, Grapes
Instructions: Chop all fruits, mix them together, and serve fresh.

Lemon Juice
Ingredients: Lemon, Water, Sugar
Instructions: Mix lemon juice, water, and sugar. Stir well and serve chilled.

Pancakes
Ingredients: Flour, Milk, Egg, Sugar
Instructions: Mix all ingredients into a batter and cook on a hot pan until golden.

Vegetable Soup
Ingredients: Carrot, Potato, Onion, Water, Salt
Instructions: Boil all vegetables until soft, season with salt, and serve hot.
